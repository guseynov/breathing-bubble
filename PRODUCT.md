# Product

## Register

product

## Users

People who want a short breathing reset on desktop or mobile, usually between tasks or during a moment of stress. They need the exercise to feel immediate, simple, and trustworthy without having to learn a new workflow.

## Product Purpose

Breathing Bubble is a browser-based guided breathing tool built around a single animated orb. It supports preset breathing patterns plus pause and resume controls, with the goal of helping users reset quickly and return to work or daily life with less friction.

## Brand Personality

Steady, restorative, precise.

The voice should feel calm and grounded, with a quiet confidence that reduces effort instead of adding ceremony. It should reassure the user, not perform for them.

## Anti-references

Avoid gamified wellness UIs, neon sci-fi atmospheres, noisy dashboard metaphors, and meditation-app clichés that make the product feel spiritualized or overdesigned. Avoid copy that is vague, inflated, or tries to sound profound.

## Design Principles

- The primary interaction should stay obvious at a glance.
- Reduce cognitive load; every control must earn its place.
- Motion should support rhythm and state, not become decoration.
- Preserve a calm, dependable experience across desktop and mobile.
- Keep guidance direct enough that the user can start immediately.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast at minimum.
Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` so the breathing experience remains usable without animation.
Do not rely on color alone to communicate state or progress.
